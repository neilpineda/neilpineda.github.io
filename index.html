Who am I?

Hello! My name is Neil Pineda, I'm a Philippine-based freelance Front-End Web Developer. I have a diverse set of skills ranging from HTML + CSS + Javascript/jQuery along with my framework of choice, Bootstrap and pre-processor, SASS/SCSS.

My scope of expertise is mainly in front end web development, focused on delivering high-class markups, coding multiple layouts, landing pages and animations to provide an interactive user experience.

I can convert any design file: PSD (Photoshop), Sketch, Ai (Illustrator), Adobe XD to pixel perfect, semantic and standard compliant HTML/CSS template. Also, I can turn your designs into high-quality, fully functional CMS driven websites based on WordPress.

Let's turn your ideas into codes, looking forward to hearing from you! :)
